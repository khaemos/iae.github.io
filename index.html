Mission

Turn electric and magnetic field behavior into engineered capability.

The institute studies electromagnetic phenomena as working principles: resonance, induction, propagation, coupling, shielding, energy transfer, sensing, and material response.

Its purpose is to keep a rigorous public record of experiments and turn promising observations into useful devices, educational reports, and repeatable laboratory methods.
